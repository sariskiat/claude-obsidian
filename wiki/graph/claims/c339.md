DNO computes criteria on exact x after full denoising, unlike guided diffusion which computes on expected E[x_0|x_t] with approximation error.

> [!quote]
> For guided diffusion methods, the criterion is computed on an expected x̂=E[x_0|x_t]... resulting in severe error when Var[x_0|x_t] is large. For DNO, the criterion is exactly computed on x after the full-chain denoising
