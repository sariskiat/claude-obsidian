MoLE eliminates the need to load expert parameters during inference by replacing expert FFNs with pre-computed lookup tables.

> [!quote]
> the experts can be reparameterized as lookup tables (LUTs) that retrieves expert outputs based on input ids, and offloaded to storage devices. Therefore, we do not need to perform expert computations during inference.
