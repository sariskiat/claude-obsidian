# Universal Inverse Distillation for Matching Models with Real-Data Supervision (No GANs)

## [Introduction] Abstract
Presents RealUID, universal distillation framework for all matching models.

## [Introduction] 1 Introduction
Matching models suffer from slow inference. Distillation methods are framework-specific.

## [Background] 2 Backgrounds on Training and Distilling Matching Models
Reviews diffusion, flow matching, UM loss, prior distillation.

## [Methods] 3 Universal Distillation of Matching Models with Real Data
UID via linearization, unifies SID FGM IBMD, extends to RealUID.

## [Evaluation] 4 Experiments
Best FID 1.98 unconditional, 1.87 conditional on CIFAR-10.

## [Discussion] 5 Discussion and Extensions
Extension beyond inverse optimization, non-flow architectures.
