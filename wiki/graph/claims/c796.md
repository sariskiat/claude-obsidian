In the RFNN model, tau_gen relates to the bulk rho_2 (order 1/Delta_t, independent of n) and tau_mem relates to the bulk rho_1 (scaling as psi_n/Delta_t, with n).

> [!quote]
> The timescale tau_gen on which the first relaxation takes place is associated to the formation of the generalization regime. It is related to the bulk rho_2 and is of order 1/Delta_t. The timescale tau_mem, on which the second stage of the dynamics takes place, is associated to overfitting and memorization. It is related to the bulk rho_1, and scales as psi_p/Delta_t*lambda_min
